• Infrastructure

An imposing and impressive Administrative block is available here that houses the Principal’s Office and the Teacher’s room along with the Accounts Department, Reception and Visitor’s room. The Academic block houses more than 40 spacious classrooms which includes Shishu Mandir and Vidya Mandir faculty with modern comfortable furniture and green boards and all other state-of-the-art activites. A beautiful and spacious library and E-library with around 10,000 books, an Art Music Rooms with wide range of Indian and Western instruments, Two Computer Labs with over 160 Computers, Centrall Hall, a Basketball Court, Tennis Court, large Football and Cricket Field, for kids are available here. Adding grace and beauty to all this is a colourful Children’s Park, that enhance the aesthetics of the surroundings with smooth manicured lawns all around
